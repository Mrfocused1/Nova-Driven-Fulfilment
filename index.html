About Us

A Trusted Partner for Growing Businesses

Nova Driven Fulfilment was founded by two brothers, Ade and Donovan, with a shared vision: to help e-commerce brands scale with confidence.

Having built and grown their own successful e-commerce venture in the fitness space, they experienced first-hand the challenges that come with rapid growth — from operational bottlenecks to the pressures of fulfilling increasing order volumes. What started as their own struggle quickly became their purpose. They realised that many brands around the world face the same growing pains, and that they had the experience and insight to make a difference.

Fast forward to today, Nova Driven Fulfilment exists to be more than just a logistics provider — we aim to be a trusted 3PL partner to ambitious e-commerce brands.

Work With Us

Despite our name, we're not a large, faceless operation. We are a close-knit team of logistics professionals with over 45 years of combined experience, united by a shared passion for delivering excellence. We understand the highs and lows of outsourcing fulfilment because we've lived it ourselves.

That's why everything we do is built around reliability, transparency, and genuine partnership. We don't just store and ship products — we help power your growth, every step of the way.
